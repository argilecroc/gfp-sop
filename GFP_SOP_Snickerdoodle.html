Soft centers, cinnamon crust, a little tang from the sourdough discard. The underdog cookie that always steals the show.

Notes

Altitude Adjustment
If cookies crater instead of crinkle, reduce baking powder to ¼ tsp next batch. The dough is already adjusted for 6,200 ft.
The Chill Is Not Optional
60 minutes minimum. Cold dough holds its shape in the oven long enough to get structure before it spreads.
The Cardamom
Nobody will name it. But they'll ask what's different. That's the cardamom. ½ tsp is the sweet spot.
Freezing for Orders
Freeze unbaked, rolled dough balls after the chill. Bake from frozen at 325°F, add 2–3 min. Never freeze finished cookies.
The one rule: Natural window light, clear counter, phone lens clean. You don't need a setup — you need good light and one subject. Six shots total. You don't have to get them all in one bake.
